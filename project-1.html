MAD

Brand identity for a design university.

Brief

Making the decisions an art director would make when creating a brand for a design university.

Creating the brand tone, the visual identity, and all the pieces that would make up the the whole brand.

Project

MAD University is a branding project for a fictional design school that aims to attract excellence.

The project emphasizes the university's commitment to creative and design-focused education, with every visual element carefully crafted to reflect the institution's values and philosophy.

Pieces

  • Brand book
  • Promotional video
  • Interactive presentation
  • Brochure
  • Campus design
