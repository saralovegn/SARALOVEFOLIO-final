HEY, WASSUP :)

My name is Sara Love Gancedo. I am half American, half Spanish, and a passionate graphic design and illustration student. My work navigates from bold colors and maximalist designs to more minimalistic magical, and emotional aesthetics.

I am deeply interested in all areas of creativity and enjoy dedicating myself fully to every project I take on. I do not have a defined style; instead, I adapt my approach to suit the unique needs of each project. I love experimenting and finding different ways to express ideas visually. I cannot commit to one specific style.
