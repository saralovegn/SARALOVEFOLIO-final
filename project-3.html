Mark Manson Book

Book design project for 3 of the Mark Manson books.

Brief

Making a photographic project about any topic we like.

Being able of capturing the essence of the subject through photography and creating a coherent narrative.

Project

I chose Torres Blancas, an iconic building in Madrid designed by architect Francisco Javier Sáenz de Oiza.

The project consists of a photobook inspired by Japanese aesthetics, focusing on minimalism, nature, and the interplay of light and shadow.
