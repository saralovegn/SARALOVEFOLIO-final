Mark Manson Book

Book design project for 3 of the Mark Manson books.

Brief

Creating the cover, backcover, and interior design for three existing books.

Incorporating a die cut element on the cover and makiking it make sense.

Project

The books I chose are: "The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck", "Everything is F*cked: A Book About Hope" and "Life, Love, Books".

Simple yet eye-catching designs that represent the essence of Mark Manson's writing style.

Pieces

  • Book cover and back cover design
  • Book editorial layout inside
